Array ADT
=====


**Array Merging**

O(M+N)- Notation means merging





**Sets**:

Union - unsorted 0(n^2) or 0(m*n^2) sorted  0(m+n)  or 0(n)

Intersection - unsorted 0(n^2) or 0(m*n^2) sorted  0(m+n) or 0(n)
